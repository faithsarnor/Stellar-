Frequently Asked Questions

What is Stellar?

Stellar is your virtual skincare concierge offering curated, dermatologist-approved product recommendations.

Are your recommendations safe for all skin types?

Yes! We recommend products suitable for sensitive, oily, dry, combination, and acne-prone skin types.

Do you sell products?

Nope! We showcase stellar skincare options and where to get them, but we don’t handle transactions.

Are your recommendations cruelty-free or vegan?

Many of them are! Look out for cruelty-free or vegan tags on our suggestions.

Can I return a product you recommended?

Returns are processed through the retailer you purchase from. We don't sell directly.

How do I know which product is right for me?

We provide detailed descriptions and a skin quiz to help you find the perfect match.

Where can I get in touch for help?

Email us at support@stellarskin.com or chat with GlamBot directly on our site!
