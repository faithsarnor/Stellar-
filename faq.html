Welcome to our FAQ section! Here, you’ll find answers to common questions about our skincare recommendations, product ingredients, and how to use them. We don’t sell directly — we guide, glow, and glam. ✨ Need help? GlamBot’s got you covered!

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What is Stellar?
A: Stellar is your virtual skincare concierge, offering curated recommendations of dermatologist-approved products for different skin types and concerns.

Q: Are your recommendations safe for all skin types?
A: Yes! We suggest products suitable for sensitive, oily, dry, combination, and acne-prone skin. Always double-check the labels and consult a dermatologist if unsure.

Q: Are the products you showcase cruelty-free or vegan?
A: Many of them are! We highlight cruelty-free or vegan tags where applicable in our recommendations.

Orders & Info

Q: Can I buy products directly from your website?
A: Not at the moment. We showcase top-rated products and provide links or guidance on where to find them, along with approximate prices to help you plan your skincare routine.

Q: Do you display actual product prices?
A: We show estimated prices to give you a sense of affordability. Prices may vary based on location or retailer.

Q: Do you ship skincare products?
A: Nope! We’re not a store – think of us more like your skincare-savvy bestie pointing you in the right direction.

Returns & Clarifications

Q: What if I have a bad reaction to a product you recommended?
A: We always recommend doing a patch test first. While we try to match products to your skin type, we can’t guarantee personal results. Always read full ingredient lists.

Q: How do I return a product?
A: Returns would be handled through the store or website you purchase the product from. We don’t process purchases or returns directly.

Product Information & Usage

Q: How do I know which product is right for me?
A: Check our skin-type tags, product reviews, and ingredient breakdowns – or take our skin quiz! GlamBot is always here to help too.

Q: Do your recommendations include dermatologist-tested items?
A: Yes, we prioritize dermatologist-recommended and tested products wherever possible. That’s part of what makes us Stellar!

Q: Do the products contain harmful ingredients?
A: Many of the brands we highlight offer clean beauty options free from parabens, sulfates, and artificial fragrances. Always check ingredient lists before use.

Contact & Support

Q: How can I contact someone for help?
A: Reach out to us at support@stellarskin.com or chat with GlamBot anytime!

Q: Do you offer skincare consultations?
A: Our AI-powered GlamBot gives quick recommendations, but for serious concerns, we advise speaking with a licensed dermatologist.
